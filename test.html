The origin of The Relationship Model

Back in 2005 I was about to get married to Danielle, my girlfriend of 2 years, and I wanted to find a way to make sure my marriage lasted. I had not seen too many examples of Great Marriages. The Great Marriages I saw did not have a “playbook” on how to get there. I felt lost and nervous. I took my fear to my close mentor. Together we built and tested the model. Then I took it to Danielle — we discussed, made changes, and committed to running our marriage according to this framework.

Over the past 20 years we shared it with a few people, and the feedback was overwhelming: “You need to share this with more people.” This is why The Relationship Model exists today.
