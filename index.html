What is really holding you back?

It's time to get real this is your life we are talking about. Doing this work is hard. In some cases there is pain and betrayal. Sometimes it might be too easy to leave it rather than address it. No one ever taught us this “stuff”.

But you see it all around you:

  • Some people seem to get lucky in a relationship
  • All the “good” guys and gals are taken
  • Others just look so happy they seem to have it all together

Relationships are supposed to be: beach holidays, great parties, beautiful clothing, smiles, cuddling, great photographs... but deep down you know it's deeper.
